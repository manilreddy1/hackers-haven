Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP)

The Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP) is one of the most respected certifications in cybersecurity. It focuses on advanced penetration testing, real-world attack techniques, and problem-solving skills in a hands-on environment. By earning this certification, you'll prove your ability to breach defenses, think like a hacker, and secure systems effectively.
