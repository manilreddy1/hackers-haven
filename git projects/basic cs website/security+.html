CompTIA Security+

The CompTIA Security+ certification is a globally recognized credential that validates your foundational knowledge in cybersecurity. It covers essential skills such as threat analysis, risk management, network security, and compliance. Security+ is ideal for beginners starting their journey in cybersecurity and is often a stepping stone for advanced certifications.
